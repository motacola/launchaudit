What this is

LaunchAudit is a paid website audit service for teams shipping launches, rebuilds, and migrations. You send the site URL and the expected page structure, and you get a discrepancy report showing what’s missing, broken, mismatched, or needs review before sign-off.

What you send

Your live site URL plus the expected pages, sitemap, or content map you want checked.

What you get

A structured discrepancy report covering missing pages, broken URLs, mismatches, content gaps, and review-required issues.

Best fit

Agencies, SEO/content teams, launch QA workflows, and migration or rebuild projects that need confidence before go-live.

What the audit catches

LaunchAudit compares your live site against the structure you expected to ship and returns a clean discrepancy report.

Missing pages

Pages that should exist but never made it live.

Broken pages

Expected URLs that exist but return bad responses or errors.

Title mismatches

Pages whose metadata or headings don’t line up with the expected page intent.

Content gaps

Signals that key expected content isn’t represented cleanly on the live site.

Review-required pages

Ambiguous or low-confidence matches that need a human check before sign-off.

Structure drift

Places where the content map and the live implementation no longer align.
